The Crisis

Billions of pens.
No plan for them.

India's education sector alone generates hundreds of millions of spent pens each year. Made from mixed plastics, these pens are rarely recyclable through conventional channels — and almost always end up in landfill or waterways.

RePen India changes that by building the infrastructure for pen collection, processing, and responsible end-of-life management — starting with the institutions that generate the most.

The Process

Simple for you.
Meaningful for everyone.

01
Register Your Institution

Sign up in under two minutes. We work with schools, colleges, NGOs, corporates — any organisation that generates used pens.

02
Collect & Request Pickup

Set up a collection bin. When it's full, log a pickup request through your dashboard. We handle the logistics.

03
We Process & Report

Your pens are responsibly processed. You receive an impact report showing exactly how much plastic was diverted from landfill.
